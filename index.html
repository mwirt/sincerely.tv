Who we are

{% image sally-lawton.jpg class="profile-pic" %}

Sally Lawton - Co-Owner

Sally studied film at DePaul University in Chicago and started Sincerely Productions with Max shortly after graduating. In addition to making commercial work, she has curated experimental cinema screenings in Chicago and Detroit, and in 2017 finished her first feature length documentary, The Hard Earth, a profile of the Ukrainian revolution. Her work has screened at Echo Park Film Center, The Nightingale Cinema, Onion City Film Festival, and other venues across the country.

{% image max-wirt.jpg class="profile-pic" %}

Max Wirt - Co-Owner

Max founded Sincerely Productions in 2013 while living in the basement of a cinema in Chicago, IL. Prior to that, he worked in Theatre and holds a BA in Theatre Directing from UCLA. In addition to producing and editing much of Sincerely's video work, he does all of the web design for the company. He moved to Detroit in 2017.

{% image caitlin-murphy.jpg class="profile-pic" %}

Caitlin Murphy - Outreach Producer

Caitlin joined Sincerely Productions in September of 2017. She brings earnestness and social responsibility to her role as a producer with Sincerely. She also works full time in Detroit's community development sector and holds professional degrees from University of Michigan and University of Detroit Mercy.
